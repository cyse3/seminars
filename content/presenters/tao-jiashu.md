Jiashu Tao is a final-year PhD student at the National University of Singapore, advised by Associate Professor Reza Shokri. His research interests lie in trustworthy machine learning, with a particular focus on privacy. His recent work centers on improving privacy auditing frameworks by designing stronger statistical tests and refining formal privacy definitions.
