Berry Schoenmakers is an associate professor in Coding Theory and Cryptology at Eindhoven University of Technology. His research centres on privacy‑preserving cryptographic protocols, especially for electronic voting, secure multiparty computation and anonymous payments. He also teaches a popular "Cryptographic Protocols" course at TU/e.
